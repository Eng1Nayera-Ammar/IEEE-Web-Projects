About Us

Our Mission

HopeMind is a unique medical care excellence Hospital.
At HopeMind hospital we are focused on providing top medical care to patients
while maintaining the highest levels of medical and ethical standards.
Our mission is to provide high quality,cost effective medical care
which surpasses the needs and expectations of patients
through comprehensive care services for inpatients and out patients as well.
The hospital is a unique facility providing state of the art diagnostic & therapeutic equipment
within our clinical areas, emergency room, operating rooms,
and intensive care unit amongst other areas.
We have recruited highly qualified medical, nursing, & supporting staff.
We are proud of our staff members who works hard
to provide the highest quality medical care,
together with a warm and caring attitude.

Our Vision

To provide superior medical and surgical care in a compassionate,
cost effective manner. To be a team of leaders recognized
for our outstanding medical care,
excellent customer service, commitment to innovation,
and reputation as the employer of choice.

Our Values

We strive to adhere to the following values as we pursue our mission and vision.
We are committed to treating our patients with dignity and respect,
and advocate responsive patient care and customer service.
We strive to offer the highest quality of care, while maintaining
the highest clinical standards. We work as a coordinated team,
sharing responsibility for our patients and our business.
We are dedicated to continuous quality improvement,
and seek out opportunities for positive change.
We have a tradition of stability and longevity, which we achieve
by hiring highly qualified staff and pursuing optimal working conditions.
